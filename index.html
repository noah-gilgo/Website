Who I Am

My name is Noah Gilgo, as you could likely guess from the domain name of this website. I am 22 years old and like to spend most of my time coding, with the rest of my spare time spent reading, jogging, gardening, and spending time with my family. I currently live in Hampstead, a town 20 miles northeast of Wilmington, North Carolina, where I attended college at the University of North Carolina at Wilmington.

As a recent graduate from the University of North Carolina at Wilmington with a B.S. in Computer Science, I have been searching diligently for real-world experience in the field of software development. I have also been keeping old skills fresh such as Java, Python and HTML, as well as acquiring new skills such as C++, CSS, and JavaScript.

This website is my most recent project; most of my projects up to this point have revolved around scientific computing and backend development, so I wanted to start a project that would help me build upon my front-end development skills. While I have had the website for a long time, I have just recently moved it onto Bluehost and have started publishing the changes I make to it on GitHub. The earliest version of this webpage was hosted on a xampp server from an old computer in my bedroom: the original webpage can be found here. While I personally like the webpage, I feel that it's a bit chaotic in terms of structure, so I would like to start anew using more refined webpage-building principles.
